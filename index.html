Welcome to RPS Pro!

This is a classic Rock, Paper, Scissors game.

Features:

  • Themes: Switch between light and dark themes using the toggle in the top-right corner.
  • Player Name: Enter your name to personalize the scoreboard.
  • Game Modes: Choose from Endless, Best of 5, or Best of 7 game modes.
  • Persistent Scores: Your scores are saved, so you can continue your game later.

How to Play:

1. Enter your name and choose a game mode.

2. Click on one of the cards (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) to make your choice.

3. The computer will make its choice, and the winner will be announced.

4. Click "Play Again" to start a new round.
